My 3D printing journey

One of the best purchase I have made was my Ender 3 V2 Printer. It was just after my first semester in college and during quarintine due to COVID-19. I had a lot of ideas for projects but they all required some way for me to manufacture parts. My Largest 3D Print Ever - Blade of the Ruined King

500+ hours of printing; 100+ hours of sanding and painting

This was my 2021 Summer project. In 2020 and 2021, everyone was in quarintine due to COVID. Because of that people got into some hobbies. My two big hobbies were 3D printing and gaming. Specifically, I played League of Legends that year. That year, there was a new character released that I really liked. What was interesting was that he had a really big sword. After doing some pixel measurements, I figured out the sword was 10 feet long! And after that realization, I decided I wanted it.

Imagine this, someone walks into your room to talk to you, leaves but doesn't close the door. Now you are left with the impossible choice of getting out of your comfortable chair to close to door or continue to do whatever you were doing with your door open. So, I created this door closer that connects to my phone and allows me to close my door in the comfort of my bed or my chair.

The hardware includes 1 stepper motor, 1 Arduino, 1 WiFi reciever, a casing that holds the stepper motor, and a wheel. I designed the casing and wheel myself and 3D printed them.
